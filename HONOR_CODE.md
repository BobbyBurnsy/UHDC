# 🛡️ UHDC Pricing & The Honor Code

**Software:** Unified Help Desk Console (UHDC)  
**Author:** Bobby Burns  

I built the UHDC to make the lives of IT professionals easier. Having grown up experimenting with and learning from software I couldn't always afford, I believe strongly in keeping the door open for the next generation of sysadmins, homelabbers, and small community teams. 

For that reason, the entire codebase for the UHDC is public, completely unrestricted, and free of DRM or hidden telemetry. I am relying entirely on the **Honor System**. 

If this tool saves your enterprise IT department hours of work, reduces your help desk ticket times, and makes your team look like rockstars, I ask that you do the right thing and purchase the appropriate commercial license. Your support directly funds the continued development of this project.

---

### 🟢 Community Tier: Free Forever ($0)
**Who it's for:** Students, homelabbers, non-profits, and small IT teams.
* **The Rules:** You can use the UHDC completely free of charge under the GNU GPLv3 if you have 3 or fewer technicians using the console and are managing up to 1,000 endpoints.
* **The Catch:** There isn't one. Learn from the code, modify it for your environment, and enjoy.

### 🔵 Professional Tier: $499 / tech / yr
**Who it's for:** Growing IT departments, Managed Service Providers (MSPs), and commercial businesses.
* **The Rules:** If your organization exceeds 3 technicians, manages over 1,000 endpoints, or you are an MSP using the UHDC to service paying clients, you are required to purchase a Professional license for each technician utilizing the software.
* **What you get:** A waiver from GPLv3 open-source requirements, unlimited endpoint management, a commercial use certificate for compliance audits, and priority email support.

### 🟣 Custom Tier: Let's Talk
**Who it's for:** Massive, global enterprises and multi-domain forests (20,000+ endpoints).
* **The Rules:** If you are deploying the Direct-to-SQL telemetry pipeline and distributing scanning nodes across a massive corporate infrastructure, please contact me for a Custom enterprise agreement. 
* **What you get:** Dedicated deployment consultation, architecture reviews, and a dedicated account manager.
