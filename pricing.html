Licensing FAQ

Is the Community tier a limited trial?
No. The Community tier is the full, unrestricted version of the software. There are no time limits, no locked features, and no artificial delays. If your organization has fewer than 1,000 computer objects in Active Directory, it is completely free forever.
How is the endpoint limit enforced?
The UHDC operates on an honor system backed by a soft AD query. When the console launches, it runs a lightweight query to count the active computer objects in your domain. If it detects over 1,000 endpoints, it will display a polite reminder in the console output to purchase a commercial license, but it will not lock you out or break functionality during a live support call.
Do we pay per technician seat?
No. We use a "Site License" model based entirely on the size of your environment (endpoints). Whether you have 5 help desk technicians or 50, the price remains exactly the same.
What if we have more than 10,000 endpoints?
For massive enterprise environments, the background asset mapping and AD queries require custom optimization to prevent network saturation. Please contact us directly via GitHub for a custom architecture consultation and quote.
